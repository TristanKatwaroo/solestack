About Us

Solestack is the world's first classifieds service for sneakers and streetwear.

Increase Profit Margins

Eliminate unnecessary expenses like shipping fees, import fees, and duty fees. We also do not charge any transaction or processing fees!

A Platform for Exposure

Get yourself known in the reseller community. Solestack is a way to get your products into your local market without being required to do any additional outreach.

Seamless Authentication Process

Vendors can upload images of their products to be verified by our experts, all through their phones!

No Shipping Time

Never again will you have to wait in suspense for your product to arrive. All business is done in person.

Build Your Credibility

Customers can give vendors a rating after doing business. Vendors with high ratings will receive more traffic, and vendors with lower ratings risk having their accounts suspended.

Secure Transactions

Make product payments in the Solestack app. We hold the payment until the product is confirmed to be delivered by both parties.
